Alumni Mentoring Program

Bridging UP students with Bay Area professionals

View Full Program Page ↗

The UPAASF Alumni Mentoring Program pairs UP students and recent graduates with seasoned alumni professionals across the San Francisco Bay Area. Our mentors bring industry expertise, personal networks, and lived experience to help mentees navigate career decisions and unlock opportunities in one of the world's most dynamic job markets.

Since its founding, the program has grown into one of UPAASF's flagship initiatives — a testament to our community's belief that every UP graduate carries a responsibility to lift others along the way.

Program Cycle

Each cohort runs for six months, launching every January and July. Pairs are matched based on career interests, industries, and mentee goals.

🎓
🤝 One-on-One Mentorship

Direct access to an experienced professional in your target field for personalized career guidance.

🌐 Network Access

Introductions to mentor's professional network — a priceless head-start in a competitive market.

📄 Career Prep

Resume reviews, interview coaching, and application support tailored to your specific goals.

🏢 Industry Insight

Gain an honest, candid view of your target industry through real-world experience.

🎟️ UPAASF Events

Complimentary or discounted access to networking events, galas, and professional sessions.

🌏 UP Community

Join a lifelong community of fellow UP scholars navigating similar journeys in the Bay Area.

Ready to Apply?

Applications for the next cohort open soon. Join over 200 mentorship pairs who've transformed their careers through this program.

Filipino Cultural Immersion Camp

Passing Filipino Heritage to the Next Generation

📄 Full Page Coming Soon
🎭

For over two decades, the UPAASF Annual Filipino Cultural Immersion Camp has been a cornerstone of our mission to preserve and celebrate Filipino culture in the Bay Area. This beloved program introduces children and young adults to the rich tapestry of Filipino heritage.

2026 Camp Information

Dates: Week of July/August — TBD
Location: Venue TBD
Ages: 8–17 years old
Cost: TBD (scholarships available)
Registration Opens: Spring 2026

🗣️ Filipino Language

Basic Tagalog conversation, common phrases, and cultural context for everyday use.

💃 Traditional Arts

Folk dances like Tinikling, cultural music performances, and traditional crafts.

📚 Filipino History

Stories of Philippine heroes, historical events, and cultural evolution.

❤️ Filipino Values

Understanding bayanihan, pakikisama, respect for elders, and family bonds.

🍽️ Filipino Cuisine

Cooking demonstrations and tastings of traditional dishes and regional favorites.

🤝 Community Building

Build friendships with other Filipino-American youth and create lasting memories.

Speakers' Series

Inspiring Conversations with Leaders and Innovators

📄 Full Page Coming Soon

The UPAASF Speakers' Series brings together thought leaders, accomplished professionals, and inspiring change-makers for engaging conversations that educate, inspire, and connect our community. Our quarterly events feature presentations, panel discussions, and Q&A sessions covering diverse topics.

💡
💻 Technology & Innovation

Silicon Valley insights, emerging technologies, and digital transformation from industry leaders.

🏥 Healthcare & Science

Medical breakthroughs, public health policy, and career pathways in the healthcare sector.

📈 Business & Finance

Entrepreneurship journeys, investment strategies, and financial literacy for professionals.

🏛️ Policy & Advocacy

Immigration law, civil rights, community organizing, and the Filipino-American political voice.

🌏 Culture & Identity

Discussions on Filipino-American identity, heritage preservation, and intergenerational stories.

🌱 Sustainability

Environmental initiatives, climate advocacy, and green career opportunities in the Bay Area.

UP in the Streets

Celebrating UP Pride and Filipino Culture in Our Communities

📄 Full Page Coming Soon
🎨

UP in the Streets is UPAASF's community engagement initiative that brings Filipino culture, UP pride, and alumni energy directly into the neighborhoods and public spaces of the Bay Area. Through art, performance, and community service, we make our presence felt and our values visible.

🖼️ Public Art Projects

Murals, installations, and exhibits in public spaces that celebrate Filipino heritage and UP pride.

🎶 Cultural Performances

Community showcases of traditional and contemporary Filipino arts in local parks and venues.

🙌 Volunteer Service

Organized community clean-ups, food drives, and neighborhood improvement events.

🏘️ Bayanihan Outreach

Partnering with local Filipino-American organizations for collaborative community programs.
