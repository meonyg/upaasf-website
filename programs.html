Alumni Mentoring Program

Empowering the Next Generation of UP Leaders

View Full Program Page ↗

The UPAASF Alumni Mentoring Program represents a bold vision: connecting UP students in the Philippines with accomplished professionals in the San Francisco Bay Area to provide career guidance, share global perspectives, and foster the innovative thinking that will shape their futures.

Launched in November 2023, this program is the first of its kind among UP alumni chapters worldwide. Developed in partnership with UP Diliman's Office of Student Development Services, the program uses a design thinking approach that puts students' needs, desires, and challenges at the heart of the mentorship experience.

🎓
🤝 One-on-One Mentorship

Personalized career guidance conversations tailored to each student's goals and aspirations.

🧠 Design Thinking Workshops

Collaborative sessions teaching innovative problem-solving and strategic planning skills.

🌐 Network Access

Introductions to your mentor's professional network — a priceless head-start in a competitive market.

📄 Career Prep

Resume reviews, interview coaching, and application support tailored to your specific goals.

🏢 Industry Insight

Gain an honest, candid view of your target industry through real-world experience.

This program could not have come at a better time. I had some idea of where I wanted to be but was unsure of how to get there. The mentorship sessions gave me clarity and confidence.

Hannah Nicole Gaudiel BS Statistics, UP Diliman

Filipino Cultural Immersion Summer Camp

Passing Filipino Heritage to the Next Generation

📄 Full Page Coming Soon
🎭

Now in its 13th year, the UPAASF Annual Filipino Cultural Immersion Summer Camp has been a cornerstone of our mission to preserve and celebrate Filipino culture in the Bay Area. This beloved program introduces children and young adults to the rich tapestry of Filipino heritage.

2026 Camp Information
Register

Dates: Week of July — TBD
Location: Venue TBD
Ages: 8–17 years old
Cost: $350 (scholarships available)
Registration Opens: Spring 2026

🗣️ Filipino Language

Basic Tagalog conversation, common phrases, and cultural context for everyday use.

💃 Traditional Arts

Folk dances like Tinikling, cultural music performances, and traditional crafts.

📚 Filipino History

Stories of Philippine heroes, historical events, and cultural evolution.

❤️ Filipino Values

Understanding bayanihan, pakikisama, respect for elders, and family bonds.

🍽️ Filipino Cuisine

Cooking demonstrations and tastings of traditional dishes and regional favorites.

🤝 Community Building

Build friendships with other Filipino-American youth and create lasting memories.

My daughter came home from camp singing Filipino songs and teaching us Tinikling! She's so proud of her heritage now. This program is a gift to our family.

Maria Santos Parent, 3rd-year camper

Week-Long Program Highlights

  • Interactive cultural workshops led by community experts
  • Traditional dance instruction and performances
  • Filipino language classes for all levels
  • Cooking demonstrations of Filipino favorites
  • Arts and crafts inspired by Philippine traditions
  • Filipino games and recreational activities
  • Field trips to Filipino cultural sites in the Bay Area
  • Culminating performance for families and community

UP in the Streets

Celebrating UP Pride Through Community Art and Engagement

🎨

UP in the Streets brings the spirit of UP into the Bay Area community through public art projects, cultural celebrations, and community engagement initiatives that showcase Filipino pride and UP excellence.

🖼️ Public Murals

Collaborative mural projects celebrating Filipino culture and UP heritage in Bay Area communities.

🎶 Community Events

Street festivals, cultural showcases, and public celebrations of Filipino traditions.

🙌 Youth Engagement

Workshops and programs introducing young people to public art and community activism.

Public programs teaching Filipino history, arts, and cultural traditions to diverse audiences.
