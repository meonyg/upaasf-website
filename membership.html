Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UPAASF membership

Who can join UPAASF? +
Any UP graduate from any campus (Diliman, Los Baños, Manila, Visayas, Mindanao, Baguio, Open University, etc.) can join UPAASF. Current UP students are also welcome with our Student Membership tier. Friends and supporters of UP are welcome to attend events and participate in programs.
How do I renew my membership? +
You'll receive a renewal reminder email 30 days before your membership expires. You can renew online through our website, or contact our membership coordinator. Lifetime members never need to renew!
What if I'm not in the Bay Area? +
You can still join! We offer virtual events, online networking opportunities, and our newsletter keeps you connected. Many members live outside the Bay Area and participate in our programs remotely. Plus, when you visit the Bay Area, you'll have instant access to all in-person events.
Can I join if I attended a specific UP campus? +
Absolutely! UPAASF welcomes alumni from all UP campuses—Diliman, Los Baños, Manila, Visayas, Mindanao, Baguio, Open University, and any other UP campus. We celebrate the diversity of the UP system and unite all iskolar ng bayan under one community.
How are membership dues used? +
Your membership dues support: (1) Program operations including the Alumni Mentoring Program and Cultural Immersion Camp, (2) Event organization and member activities, (3) Scholarships and support for UP students, (4) Administrative costs for the association, and (5) Philanthropic initiatives supporting UP campuses and disaster relief. We publish an annual report detailing our financial stewardship.
What's the difference between membership tiers? +
All members receive core benefits like event access and networking. Higher tiers (Patron, Benefactor) provide enhanced recognition, VIP experiences, and greater impact through increased support for UP students and programs. Family memberships cover multiple people. Lifetime membership eliminates annual renewals. Choose the tier that aligns with your goals and budget.
