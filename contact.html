Quick Answers

Frequently asked questions about contacting UPAASF

What's the best way to reach UPAASF? +
Email is the most reliable way to reach us at info@upaasf.org. We respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, you can call us during business hours. You can also use the contact form on this page.
How do I inquire about membership? +
You can email our Membership Coordinator at membership@upaasf.org or visit our Membership page for complete information about joining UPAASF. We're happy to answer any questions about benefits, dues, and the application process.
Can I visit your office? +
UPAASF is primarily a volunteer-run organization without a physical office open to the public. However, we hold regular events throughout the Bay Area where you can meet board members and fellow alumni. Check our Events page for upcoming gatherings.
How can I get involved as a volunteer? +
We welcome volunteers for events, programs, and committee work. Use the contact form above and select "Volunteer Opportunities" as the subject, or email us at info@upaasf.org expressing your interest and areas of expertise.
How do I report a website issue? +
Please email info@upaasf.org with details about the issue you're experiencing, including your browser and device information. We appreciate your help in keeping our website running smoothly!
