Our Story: 50 Years of Excellence, Service, and Community

Since 1973, UPAASF has been the heart of the UP community in the San Francisco Bay Area

Who We Are

Since incorporating in 1973 in Daly City, California, the University of the Philippines Alumni Association of San Francisco has served as the heart of the UP community in the Bay Area. What began as a small gathering of UP graduates has grown into a vibrant organization of over 500 members—engineers and educators, doctors and designers, entrepreneurs and public servants—all united by our shared alma mater and Filipino heritage.

Five Decades of Impact

Our journey spans five transformative decades. We've witnessed the Bay Area's evolution into a global technology hub, celebrated the achievements of countless Filipino-American leaders, and remained steadfast in our commitment to UP and our community. In 2023, we proudly celebrated our 50th anniversary, reflecting on our legacy while looking toward an even brighter future.

Pioneering Innovation

UPAASF has always embraced innovation. In 2023, we launched the first alumni mentoring program of its kind among UP chapters worldwide—connecting UP Diliman students with Silicon Valley professionals for career guidance and design thinking sessions. This groundbreaking initiative exemplifies our commitment to bridging generations and leveraging our Bay Area expertise to empower the next generation of UP scholars.

Preserving Heritage, Building Community

Through our signature Filipino Cultural Immersion Camp, we pass Filipino traditions, language, and values to younger generations. Our cultural programs, community events, and social gatherings create spaces where UP alumni and their families connect, celebrate, and support one another through the spirit of bayanihan.

Supporting Our Alma Mater

We've never forgotten our roots. UPAASF has raised hundreds of thousands of dollars to support UP, including major contributions to the renovation of Kalayaan Residence Hall. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we stepped up to provide critical assistance to the Philippine General Hospital, supporting frontline healthcare workers when they needed it most.

Leading with Purpose

Today, UPAASF stands as UP's premier alumni organization in the San Francisco Bay Area. We lead with the values that define our university—excellence, honor, service—while embracing the spirit of innovation that defines our region. Whether you graduated decades ago or just joined us from Diliman, Los Baños, Manila, or any UP campus, you have a home here.

This is more than an alumni association. This is family.

Our Core Values

These pillars guide everything we do—from mentoring students to preserving culture, from hosting events to supporting our alma mater.

Honor & Excellence

Husay at Dangal

We uphold the highest standards of academic excellence and personal integrity that define the University of the Philippines. Every action reflects our commitment to the UP tradition of producing leaders who serve with distinction.

Leadership & Service

Panugo at Lingkod

We lead through service, mentoring the next generation and giving back to our communities. True leadership means lifting others, supporting our alma mater, and contributing to societal advancement.

Community & Culture

Bayanihan at Kultura

We celebrate Filipino heritage through bayanihan—the spirit of communal unity and cooperation. Our cultural programs preserve traditions, strengthen bonds, and pass our values to future generations.

Our Journey Through the Years

Five decades of community building, cultural preservation, and service to UP

1973
UPAASF Incorporated

Founded in Daly City, California, establishing the first formal UP alumni organization in the San Francisco Bay Area.

1990s
Cultural Programs Launch

Established the Annual Filipino Cultural Immersion Camp, introducing generations of youth to Filipino heritage and traditions.

2000s
Kalayaan Hall Support

Major fundraising campaign supporting the renovation of Kalayaan Residence Hall at UP Diliman, providing a home for freshman students.

2020
COVID-19 Relief Efforts

Provided critical assistance to Philippine General Hospital, supporting frontline healthcare workers during the pandemic.

2023
50th Anniversary & Mentoring Program Launch

Celebrated golden anniversary and launched the groundbreaking Alumni Mentoring Program—the first of its kind among UP chapters worldwide.

2026
Looking Forward

Continuing our mission to connect alumni, empower students, and preserve Filipino culture for the next generation.
